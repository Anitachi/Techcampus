Cosmetics Market, Ojota, Lagos-Nigeria

My travel tour to ojota market

Where cosmetics skincare products are sold such as organic and non organic products

This is a symbolic picture of how the market is usually crowded most especially on weekends or on a public holiday The Ojota Market A market full of any skincare products for both retailers and manucfacturers.
Today Ojota is said to be the market in the Nigeria where organic products are sourced from for production and been transported to other states if needed by skincare vendors not in state (Lagos) In 2010, it is also nicknamed as the Organic Skincare Market
